# Mise en Place :neofox_floof_mug:

*Everything in its place  –  before the cooking starts!*

A good chef preps the station first. This section is about the **tools and
places** you cook with **fenn**ecs in  –  setup and install guides for the
utensils around your code, rather than recipes for the code itself:

- 🤖 [**Agents**](Agents.md)  –  hand your AI pair-programmer the official
  **fenn**ecs skill.
- 🖥️ **IDEs**  –  Rider, Visual Studio, VS Code... install guides are on the
  stove and coming soon.
- 🎮 **Engines**  –  in the meantime, the [Demos](/examples/index) show
  **fenn**ecs plugged into Godot, Stride, MonoGame, Raylib, and friends.

*Missing a pot or pan? Tell us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/3SF4gWhANS)!*
